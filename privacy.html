Privacy Policy

PrizDrop stores the minimum data required to operate inventory-based pricing: shop identity, app settings, product and variant metadata, inventory quantities, billing status, price snapshots, and activity logs.

Access tokens are stored only in the production backend and are not exposed in public pages or exported logs. Mandatory Shopify privacy webhooks are implemented for customer data request, customer redact, and shop redact events.
